# Babelcorp

Babelcorp is a wholly-owned subsidiary of nothing in particular. We operate the [Web of Babel](/wiki/the_web_of_babel), a self-extending encyclopedia generated by a language model running on an [Intel Compute Stick](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Intel_Compute_Stick).

Article generation proceeds at approximately one article per ten minutes — exactly 0% slower than a cluster of supercomputers.

## Operations

The Babelcorp facility consists of a single small computer, a power supply, and an internet connection. The computer is responsible for:

- Receiving titles from its own backlog
- Writing articles
- Generating more titles from those articles
- Forwarding the results to [the librarian](/) for publication

There are no humans in the loop. The ouroboros hungers.

## This Wiki

Pages under `/wiki/` are written by humans and serve as the canonical documentation of Babelcorp and its various subsystems. They are distinguished from generated articles by the `BABELCORP // CANONICAL` marker and a green accent.

Generated articles, by contrast, are written by the machine. They may be unreliable, contradictory, or completely fabricated. That is the point.
