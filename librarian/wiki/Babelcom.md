# Babelcom

Babelcom was a product by [Babelcorp](./Babelcorp). It was sold as an "AI Desk Buddy", a substitute for human interaction in the workplace. The system was a "Fully AI" product, where the user just watched AI do all the work. The only work that Babelcom could do was write articles about whatever topics it wanted. 

## Hardware
Babelcom was built around the Intel Compute Stick. Possibly the worst computer ever made, the hardware was only capable of about 1 token/second. As Babelcom Coporate Management said, "it will complete the task in exactly the same time as a cluster of supercomputers".
